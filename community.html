CAMPS

At Reach Personal Training, we believe that proper health and exercise is fundamental for the whole family to reach their personal best. Injuries to ACL, labrum (hip), and rotator cuff are on the rise, specifically among youth. Two major causes are that children are specializing in a single sport at younger ages and are not learning how to move through a full range of motion. The result? Injuries!

Based on our philosophies and our first hand knowledge on youth injuries, we introduced kid camps for the summer of 2013 with the following goals:

  • Improve self-esteem for boys and girls
  • Strengthen to prevent injury
  • Increase flexibility
  • Teach and encourage healthy eating
  • Have fun in a productive environment

We will be offering two one week sessions June 17 – 21 and June 24 – 28, 2013 for children 10 and up. Days will begin at 10:00 am and run until 3:00 pm. Twice a day training with a flexibility modality will provide for very quick gains in a short time.

Our daily schedule consists of below:

10:00 Arrival
10:15 Lesson – overview of the day’s activities
10:30 Morning Training – strength, plyometrics, technique and mobility
11:30 Protein Shake
12:00 Lunch
1:15 Stretch
2:00 Afternoon Training – energy systems, modified strong man training
3:00 Protein Shake
