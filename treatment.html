NATUROPATHY

Naturopathic doctors are trained as primary care physicians and are able to treat a wide variety of health concerns from acute to chronic, pediatric to geriatric, and physical to psychological. In the absence of a particular concern or illness, naturopaths can also offer strategies for disease prevention and health promotion.

Naturopathic doctors are trained in a wide variety of treatment options, otherwise known as modalities. The most commonly used modalities are: clinical nutrition, lifestyle counselling, botanical medicine, Chinese medicine and homeopathy.

Conditions that are treated include but are not limited to:

  • Menstrual Concerns
  • Men’s and Women’s Health
  • Digestive Concerns
  • Allergies and Food Sensitivities
  • Immune System Health
  • Cardiovascular Health
  • Inflammatory Conditions
  • Pain Management
  • Detoxification and Weight Management
  • Stress Management
